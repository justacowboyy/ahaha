About Luma AI

Luma AI solves two main challenges in movie search:

  1. Constraint-based Search (Cypher): Perfect for specific queries with multiple conditions, when you're restricted to certain time duration or certain genre. Example: Finding Tarantino action movies from the 2010s under 160 minutes.
    • Tech stack used: Knowledge graph database and GraphRAG
  2. Hybrid-Similarity Search (Hybrid): Ideal when you can't remember the exact title. Searches through movie titles and descriptions using advanced similarity matching. Example : Movies with apes.
    • Tech stack used: Pinecone database and Hybrid search: BM25 encoder + Semantic search
